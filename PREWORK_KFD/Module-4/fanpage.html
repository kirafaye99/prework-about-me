Harry Styles Biography

Harry Styles got his start to fame when he went on the UK X-Factor. He then got grouped up with 4 other boys who would create the boy band One Direction. After One Direction broke up in 2015, Harry Styles began his successful solo career in which he made many singles and two albums.
